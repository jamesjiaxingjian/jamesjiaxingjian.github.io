Biography
=====
Biography
Jiaxing Jian received a Master’s degree in Linguistics from Peking University, where he also got intensive academic training in Chinese Indonesian history. He received his Bachelor’s degree in Indonesian from Shanghai International Studies University. He is currently a PhD student at the University of Sydney. His research interests include translocal Chinese Indonesians and Chinese communities in Southeast Asia, with a focus on culture, identity, history, and languages.
